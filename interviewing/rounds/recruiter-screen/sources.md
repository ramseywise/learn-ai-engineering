## External — Comp Research

| Resource | Use |
|----------|-----|
| [levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi) | Most reliable source for TC (total comp) by company, level, and location for software/ML/DS roles. Filter by role type and YOE. Use the range, not a single number. |
| [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com) | Salary ranges and offer data (less precise than levels, more coverage for non-FAANG). Also useful for company culture signal and recruiter review patterns. |
| [Blind](https://www.teamblind.com) | Community comp data and offer comparison threads. Higher variance but useful for outliers. Filter for recency — 2023+ data only. |
| [LinkedIn Salary](https://www.linkedin.com/salary/) | Broad coverage, lower precision. Useful for non-tech-hub markets and roles outside the standard FAANG ladder. |

## Note on this round

This is the one round where external prep materials matter less than internal rehearsal. The 90-second narrative is not improved by reading about it — it improves by saying it out loud three times and then adjusting. Use the study guide to build the material; use a recording or a mirror to rehearse it.
