# Recruiter / HR Screen

## What's tested

Narrative coherence and mutual fit — not technical depth. The recruiter is answering two questions:

1. Can you tell a coherent story about who you are, why this role, and why now — in under two minutes?
2. Do logistics (comp, timeline, location, authorization) rule you out before anyone technical spends an hour on you?

Communication and enthusiasm are scored. Your story is pattern-matched against the req.

## Format

- **Duration**: 15–30 minutes
- **Medium**: Phone or video call; occasionally async screening questions
- **Stage**: Always first — universally the entry gate to every loop
- **Interviewer**: Recruiter or HR coordinator, not a hiring manager or engineer

## Prep checklist

- [ ] 90-second narrative ready and rehearsed out loud (past → present → this role)
- [ ] One-line summaries of 2–3 flagship projects
- [ ] Comp range researched and framed as a range, not a number
- [ ] Logistics pre-decided: notice period, hybrid/remote constraints, authorization, other processes in flight
- [ ] 2–3 questions for them: team size, loop structure, timeline to decision
- [ ] Concrete AI-tool fluency answer prepared (2026: recruiters probe this directly)

## Per-role weighting

| AIE | MLE | DS | FDE |
|-----|-----|----|-----|
| ●   | ●   | ●  | ●   |

Universal first gate. FDE screens lean harder on communication polish. All roles are equal here — this round is about story and fit, not technical stack.
